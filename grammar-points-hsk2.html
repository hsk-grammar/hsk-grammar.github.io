  17. Reduplication of Measure Words

    Measure words can be reduplicated [and usually followed by 都 (dōu)] to give meaning of each/every. Examples:
    同学们个个都很高兴 (tóng xué men gè gè dōu hěn gāo xìng) - The students are all very happy.
    这个商店的衣服件件都很漂亮 (zhè ge shāng diàn de yī fú jiàn jiàn dōu hěn piào liang) - All the clothes in this shop are very beautiful.
    这个星期我天天都吃羊肉 (zhè ge xīng qí wǒ tiān tiān doū chī yáng ròu) - I ate lamb every day this week.

  22. The Modal Particle 呢 (ne)

    ne can be added to the end of a statement to add emphasis or insistence to your statement. Examples:
    八点上课，时间还早呢 (bā diǎn shàng kè, shí jiān hái zǎo ne) - Class starts at 8, that's so early!
    医院离我们这儿还远呢 (yī yuàn lí wǒ men zhèr hái yuǎn ne) - The hospital is so far away from here.
    做公共汽车要一个多小时呢 (zuò gōng gòng qì chē yào yī gè duō xiǎo shí ne) - To go by bus will take more than an hour!

  33. The 比 (bǐ) Sentence (1)

    Basic comparisons with "bi"
    Basic comparisons with "meiyou"
    Note: to say something is a "a bit more" you can put yi dian after the adjective. To say something is "a lot more" you can add hen duo after the adjective. You can also use a specific quantity to specify exactly how much more. Examples:
    西瓜比苹果贵五块钱 (xī guā bǐ píng guǒ guì wǔ kuài qián) - Watermelon is 5 RMB more expensive than apples.
    我的学习比他的好有一点儿 (wǒ de xué xí bǐ tā de hǎo yǒu yī diǎnr) - My studies are a bit better than his.
    今天比昨天热很多 (jīn tiān bǐ zuó tiān rè hěn duō) - Today is much hotter than yesterday.
    她比我小两岁 (tā bǐ wǒ xiǎo liǎng suì) - She is two years younger than me.

  36. The 比 (bǐ) Sentence (2)

    The bi construction can also be used to compare how two people perform an action using degree complements. Examples:
    他比我学得好 (tā bǐ wǒ xué dé hǎo) / 他学得比我好 (tā xué dé bǐ wǒ hǎo) - He studies better than I do.
    姐姐比我跑得快 (jiě jiě bǐ wǒ pǎo dé kuài) / 姐姐跑得比我快 (jiě jiě pǎo dé bǐ wǒ kuài) - My older sister runs faster than I do.
    我妻子比我做得好 (wǒ qī zi bǐ wǒ zuò dé hǎo) / 我妻子做得比我好 (wǒ qī zi zuò dé bǐ wǒ hǎo) - My wife cooks better than me.
